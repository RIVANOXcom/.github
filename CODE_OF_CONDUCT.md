# Code of Conduct

RIVANOX public repositories are intended for constructive technical feedback, product questions, issue reports and improvement ideas.

Please keep discussions:

- respectful
- relevant to the product or documentation
- clear and actionable
- free of spam, harassment, hate speech or personal attacks

Public repositories are not a place for sharing license keys, private customer data, credentials, confidential repository contents or security-sensitive details.

Maintainers may close, edit, lock or remove discussions that are abusive, off-topic, spam-like, unsafe or legally problematic.
